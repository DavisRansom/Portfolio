About Me

Growing up as an athlete, I had dreams of becoming a professional volleyball player and achieved a degree of success playing semi-pro, but a serious back injury ended my chances of playing at a high level. So, I went into teaching and coaching and have spent the past 25 years doing so in various capacities.


During the pandemic my priorities shifted to focusing on what I really wanted to do with the next portion of my life and I started becoming super interested in technology. So, after about a year and a half of thinking about it, I decided to jump into UCSD's Full-stack Web Development Bootcamp and I have started making some projects!
